Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. In today’s connected world, where most activities happen online, cybersecurity plays a crucial role in safeguarding sensitive information. It involves techniques like encryption, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems to prevent unauthorized access. Organizations invest heavily in cybersecurity to protect customer data and maintain trust. Individuals also need to follow safe practices like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links. As cyber threats continue to evolve, staying informed and adopting robust security measures is essential to ensure digital safety and privacy.
