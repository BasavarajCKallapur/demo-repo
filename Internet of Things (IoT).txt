The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of interconnected devices that communicate and share data over the internet. These devices include smart home appliances, wearable fitness trackers, and industrial sensors. IoT improves efficiency by automating tasks and enabling real-time monitoring. For example, smart thermostats can adjust temperature based on user preferences, while industrial IoT systems can predict equipment failures. However, IoT also raises concerns about data security and privacy. As technology advances, IoT is expected to play a major role in creating smarter cities, improving healthcare systems, and enhancing everyday convenience.
